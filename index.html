About Me

I am an economist specializing in behavioral economics, health economics, and applied econometrics. I currently serve as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Humanities and Social Sciences at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Roorkee.

I hold a PhD (Summa Cum Laude) from the Hungarian University of Agriculture and Life Sciences (formerly Szent István University). In addition to my teaching and research, I serve as a Scientific Editor for the International Review of Economics and Finance.
